---
title: From the Mouth of a Cannibal
date: 2025-06-26
---

One late night I built up quite an appetite,
and the incessant buzzing and the yellow light,
coming from the pristine white fridge from across the room,
had me foaming at the mouth for a bite.
In hindsight it was getting far too late
but despite all the things I ate,
I stood up with my plate and in haste
dropped it on my foot.
Picking up the pieces, I cut my hand, now bleeding,
I licked off the blood to get a taste.
I did not hesitate or fight with my mind at that time,
and as if by fate I sank my teeth into my arm.
